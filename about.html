What's my story?

I'll keep it brief

Alanta is a junior UX/UI Designer from London, UK.

Originally in the motor trade as a trained paint & body vehicle repairer I decided to flip the coin and pursue where my passion laid, in design.

I started to learn front-end development with the help of SheCodes.io where I embarked on many exhilarating coding challenges however kept gravitating back to the design process. So I have started to learn and develop my skills with the help of Creative Alliance's UX/UI Bootcamp course. From here I hope to refine and improve my skills set which I'll be documenting on my portfolio.
